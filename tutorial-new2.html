Fitting Data with the Movable Line

When two numerical variables form a pattern that looks like a straight line, a linear model can help describe how they are related. Tuva's Movable Line tool lets you adjust a line directly on the graph to see how well it fits the data.

How It Works

1. Create a Scatter Plot

Drag one numerical attribute to the x-axis and another to the y-axis to visualize the relationship.

2. Add a Movable Line

From the toolbar, select the Stats or Modeling menu, then choose Movable Line. A line will appear on your graph.

3. Adjust for Best Fit

Fine-tune the line to match your data:

  • Drag the middle of the line to change the intercept.
  • Drag either end to change the slope (rotation).
  • Stretch the line to extend it across the plot.

Note: As you move the line, its equation updates automatically!
